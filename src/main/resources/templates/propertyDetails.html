Up North Lake Houses - Gaylord Snowmobile Lodging at its finest!

Start your family tradition today, by booking one of our premium lake houses. Our vacation rentals are located in Johannesburg, MI, which is just a few miles from the city of Gaylord. Each property has 3 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms, and can sleep 9 guests. Our central location also makes for a perfect (all-season) vacation destination, as we are just an hour drive to several great places to visit – such as Mackinac Island, Traverse City, and Petosky.

In addition, Gaylord is known as the Golf Mecca of the mid-west. There are over 25 golf courses nearby for the golf enthusiast. Our properties are clean and meticulously maintained. Our lake houses are situated upon amazing lakes and are near ATV, snowmobiling, biking and hiking trails. Don’t wait. Make your reservations today!

Three Bears Log Cabin
Why Us? FAQ Contact Us

6382 Beach Rd. Johannesburg, MI

Our cabin has two bedrooms, a sleeping porch and a pull-out sofa in the living room. We have slept 8 comfortably many times. All windows on sleeping porch open allowing the lake breezes to move through the cabin. It is a fight for the sleeping porch in our family. We have a great kitchen table for family gatherings or a table on the sleeping porch for you to play cards, board games, or just hang out with family and friends. Our kitchen comes fully equipped with most items needed to prepare meals. At night enjoy our fire pit area on the beach that is perfect for roasting marshmallows with a spot for everyone to huddle around the campfire.

Little Bear Lake has been recognized as one of the top ten cleanest lakes in Michigan. This makes the lake perfect for swimming, fishing, or kayaking. If you’d like you may bring your own boat, or if you don’t have your own boat Twin Lakes Marina is nearby where you can rent a boat. They rent out pontoon boats, kayaks and paddle boards.
